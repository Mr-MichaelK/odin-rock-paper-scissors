Hit Play!

How to play:
  1. Hit the play button to start a first to 5 round match.
  2. To make a choice, hit the button corresponding to the option you chose.
  3. To move on to the next round, make another choice (e.g pick scissors).
  4. Click the reset button to reset the game when it is finished.
  5. Hit the play again button to restart the game.
