Hit Play!
How to play:
  1. Hit the play button to start a 5 round match.
  2. You have 3 seconds per round to make your choice.
  3. To make a choice, hit the button corresponding to the option you chose, or type the key that represents the first letter of your choice (e.g. 'r' for rock).
  4. To move on to the next round, hit the ready button.
  5. Hit the play again button to restart the game.
